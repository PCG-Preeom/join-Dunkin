Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

Getting Started
Do I need experience to apply? ▼
No experience needed at all. We provide full training from day one — all you need is a positive attitude and willingness to learn.
How do I apply? ▼
Browse open positions on our Jobs page, click "Submit Application" on a role you like, and fill out the short form. Takes about 5 minutes.
How soon will I hear back after applying? ▼
We typically reach out within 2–3 business days. Most candidates are interviewed and hired within one week of applying.
Pay & Hours
What are the pay rates? ▼
Pay starts competitively at $13–$16/hr depending on the role, and increases with performance reviews. Shift Leaders and Managers earn more, and we offer regular raises for strong performers.
What hours and shifts are available? ▼
We offer morning, afternoon, evening, and weekend shifts. Both full-time and part-time positions are available — we work around your schedule as much as possible.
Are there any benefits? ▼
Yes — free Dunkin' beverages and food discounts on every shift, flexible scheduling, and competitive pay with regular performance reviews.
Growth & Opportunities
Are there opportunities to move up? ▼
Absolutely — we promote from within whenever possible. Many of our Shift Leaders and Managers started as Crew Members and worked their way up.
Can I work at multiple locations? ▼
Yes! With 45+ locations across PA and NJ, there are plenty of options. Talk to your manager about transfer or multi-location opportunities.
Do you hire students or people looking for part-time work? ▼
Absolutely — we love working with students. We offer flexible scheduling that can work around classes and exams. Many of our crew members are current college students.

Still have questions?

We're happy to help — reach out and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
